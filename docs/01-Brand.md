# MaxPost Brand Specification

**Version:** 1.0 Alpha  
**Status:** Living document  
**Owner:** MaxPost

## Positioning

MaxPost is an independent software studio focused on small, reliable Windows utilities. The brand competes through simplicity, trust, speed, consistency and respect for the user.

## Brand promise

Every MaxPost product should be:

- fast;
- lightweight;
- safe;
- understandable;
- visually consistent;
- honest about internet access, updates and promotions.

## Mission

Create software that saves time without demanding attention.

## Core slogans

Primary:

> One Tool. One Task. Done.

Secondary:

> Simple software that saves your time.

## Personality

MaxPost should feel like an experienced engineer: calm, direct, competent and predictable.

Use language that is:

- concise;
- technically accurate;
- plain;
- helpful;
- free of hype.

Avoid words such as “revolutionary”, “ultimate”, “industry-leading”, “best ever” and “next-generation” unless objectively supported.

## Naming

Public product names use the `MP` prefix:

- MP Folder Creator
- MP Image Converter
- MP ZIP Password Generator
- MP Bulk Rename
- MP Duplicate Finder
- MP Text to Speech

Names describe the task. Do not invent abstract product names that hide the function.

## Logo

The primary mark is a compact `MP` monogram paired with the wordmark `MaxPost`.

Rules:

- use a square monogram;
- keep the mark readable at 24 px;
- avoid bevels, glossy effects and complex decoration;
- preserve clear space equal to at least one quarter of the monogram width;
- never stretch or rotate the logo.

## Typography

Preferred family: `Inter`.

Allowed weights:

- 400 Regular
- 500 Medium
- 600 Semibold
- 700 Bold

Fallback stack:

```css
font-family: Inter, ui-sans-serif, system-ui, -apple-system, "Segoe UI", sans-serif;
```

## Iconography

Use Lucide-style outlined icons with consistent stroke width. Do not mix unrelated icon libraries in one product.

## Product imagery

The product is the hero image.

Preferred imagery:

- real application screenshots;
- clean interface mockups;
- simple vector diagrams;
- geometric gradients used sparingly.

Avoid:

- stock business photography;
- generic laptop images;
- decorative AI-generated people;
- screenshots containing personal information.

## Screenshot standard

Application screenshots should use:

- consistent window size and theme;
- clean test data;
- no desktop wallpaper;
- no visible private paths or usernames;
- WebP or AVIF for the website;
- meaningful alt text.

## Voice examples

Good:

> Create hundreds of folders in seconds.

Bad:

> Experience the ultimate revolutionary folder-management solution.

Good:

> The application checks for updates once per day. You can disable this in Settings.

Bad:

> Seamless cloud-enhanced technology keeps you ahead.

## Brand prohibitions

MaxPost must not use:

- fake urgency;
- fake download buttons;
- misleading badges;
- fabricated ratings or download counts;
- hidden promotions;
- aggressive pop-ups;
- dark patterns.

## Success signal

The desired user reaction is:

> It simply works.
